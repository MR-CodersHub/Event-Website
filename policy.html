Data Governance Protocol

Privacy
Policy

Outlining our commitment to data fidelity, encryption standards, and the discrete handling of organizational intelligence.

01. Data Collection & Intelligence Gastronomy

Lumina collects only the metrics necessary to ensure zero-latency project orchestration. This includes organizational briefing data, technical environment parameters, and authorized personnel contact vectors.

We do not operate on speculative data collection. Every data point harvested is audited for operational relevance and purged upon mission completion unless statutory retention is required.

02. Encryption & Storage Protocols

All sensitive briefing material is processed through our hardened internal infrastructure. We utilize military-grade AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 for all transient signals.

Storage nodes are globally distributed across secure data centers in Geneva, Singapore, and New York, ensuring high-availability and regional sovereignty compliance.

03. Cookie & Tracking Transmissions

Our website utilizes a minimalist cookie protocol to maintain session fidelity. We do not engage in cross-site tracking or facilitate third-party advertising cookies.

Users may opt to disable browser cookies; however, this may degrade the responsiveness of the Lumina Hub interface.

04. User Rights & Data Sovereignty

Authorized personnel retain the right to request a full transcript of their organizational data profile. Requests for data erasure are processed within 4 functional hours through our Command Center.

Lumina complies with global data protection frameworks, including GDPR and CCPA, maintaining the highest standard of international compliance.

Last Revision: January 2026

This policy is subject to real-time updates as security landscapes evolve. For specific data audits, contact ops@lumina.studio.
