The Art of Orchestration

Founded in 1998, Lumina was born from a need for greater reliability in the high-stakes world of global summits. Our founding partners, coming from backgrounds in aviation logistics and luxury hospitality, realized that the secret to a successful event was invisible infrastructure.

Today, we operate from four global hubs—Geneva, New York, Singapore, and Dubai—serving a roster of Fortune 500 companies, non-profit foundations, and private estates.

We don't just "plan" events. We engineer environments. Whether it's a shareholder meeting in London or a residential celebration in the Maldives, our commitment to absolute precision remains the same.
